상평형¶

구성 조건¶

  • $\text{X}\text{(l)}\longleftrightarrow\text{X}\text{(g)}$ .

  • $x,\,y$ $\text{X(l)},\,\text{X(g)}$ .

  • $\text{X(l)}\longrightarrow \text{X(g)}$(, evaporation) , $v_e$ $T$ $T$ . $v_e=T$

  • $\text{X(l)}\longleftarrow\text{X(g)}$(, condensation) , $v_c$ , . , $v_c$ $k$ $v_c=ky$ .

  • $x$, $y$ . $$\begin{cases}\displaystyle\frac{dx}{dt}&=v_c-v_e&=ky-T\\\displaystyle\frac{dy}{dt}&=v_e-v_c&=T-ky\end{cases}$$

용해평형¶

구성 조건¶

  • $\text{X(s)}\longleftrightarrow\text{X(aq)}$ .

  • $x$, $y$ $\text{X(s)}$ $\text{X(aq)}$ .

  • $\text{X(s)}\longrightarrow\text{X(aq)}$(, dissolution) , $v_s$ . $v_s=k_s$

  • $\text{X(s)}\longleftarrow\text{X(aq)}$(, eduction) , $v_e$ . $k_e$ $v_e=k_e y$

  • $x$, $y$ . $$\begin{cases}\displaystyle\frac{dx}{dt}&=v_e-v_s&=k_e y-k_s\\\displaystyle\frac{dy}{dt}&=v_s-v_e&=k_s-k_ey\end{cases}$$

화학평형¶

구성 조건¶

  • $2\text{A}\longleftrightarrow\text{A}_2$ .

  • $x$, $y$ $\text{A}$ $\text{A}_2$ .

  • $2\text{A}\longrightarrow\text{A}_2$ , $v_1$ $A$ . $$v_1=k_1 x$$

  • $2\text{A}\longleftarrow\text{A}_2$ , $v_2$ $A_2$ . $$v_2 = k_2 y$$

  • $x$, $y$ . $$\begin{cases} \displaystyle \frac{dx}{dt}&=2v_2-2v_1&=2k_2 y-2k_1 x\\\displaystyle\frac{dy}{dt}&=v_1-v_2&=k_1x-k_2y \end{cases}$$

동적 평형 일반화¶

구성 조건¶

  • $a\text{A}\longleftrightarrow b\text{B}$ .

  • $x$, $y$ $\text{A}$ $\text{B}$ .

  • $a\text{A}\longrightarrow b\text{B}$ , $v_1$ $A$ . $$v_1=k_1 x$$

  • $a\text{A}\longleftarrow b\text{B}$ , $v_2$ $A_2$ . $$v_2 = k_2 y$$

  • $x$, $y$ . $$\begin{cases} \displaystyle \frac{dx}{dt}&=a v_2-a v_1&=ak_2 y-ak_1 x\\\displaystyle\frac{dy}{dt}&=bv_1-bv_2&=bk_1x-bk_2y \end{cases}$$
